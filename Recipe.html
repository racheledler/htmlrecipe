Delicious Oven-Roasted Potatoes Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 lb. Red Potatoes
  • 1/4 cup Olive Oil
  • 1 tbsp. Season Salt
  • 1 tbsp. Garlic Powder
  • 1 tbsp. Italian Seasoning
  • 1 tbsp. Paprika
  • Kosher Salt
  • Ground Black Pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Cut the red potatoes into quarter sized pieces, or as desired.
  3. Place potatoes onto a baking sheet and toss the oil and seasonings until the potatoes are evenly coated.
  4. Bake in oven for 25 mintutes.
  5. Serve and enjoy.
